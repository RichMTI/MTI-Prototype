STEP 3 - Calculating the weekly binary bonus
The weekly calculation of the binary bonuses is done by the system and will be reflected in your back office at the end of the week. The trading week is from Monday to Friday and your trading bonus will appear in your back office daily. The week is concluded on a Saturday morning when the binary bonuses will be added to your account.

The following facts are used to calculate the weekly binary sharing bonuses:

Firstly the share of the bonuses will need to be calculated, as the trading bonuses are divided as follows: 40% to you the member, 30% to the Trading System, 10% to MTI - for administration & to pay the referral bonuses and 20% to the Binary Sharing Bonuses.

The 20% Binary bonus will be shared amongst you and your team weekly. The calculation of this involves the following data: The total bonuses from all members in the company, then the portion of the total business your team structure contributes. This is then calculated for every binary qualified member and then the bonuses are shared according to this and the membership qualifyng criteria in Step 2. If there are no trading bonuses for the week, there will be no binary sharing bonuses.
